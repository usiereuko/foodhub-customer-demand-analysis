# FoodHub: Customer Demand and Restaurant Analysis

Exploratory data analysis project examining customer ordering behavior, cuisine popularity, delivery performance, and business insights for a food delivery platform.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer order data from FoodHub, a food aggregation platform operating in New York. The analysis focuses on customer demand patterns, cuisine popularity, restaurant performance, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

The objective is to generate actionable business insights that can help improve operational efficiency, customer experience, and strategic decision-making.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer ordering behavior and restaurant demand patterns
- Identify the most popular cuisines and restaurants
- Explore delivery and food preparation trends
- Generate actionable business insights and recommendations

---

## Skills & Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Data Visualization
- Business Insights & Recommendations

---

## Key Insights

- American, Japanese, Italian, and Chinese cuisines emerged as the most popular customer choices.
- Most customer orders ranged between \$11 and \$13.
- Orders were more frequent on weekends than weekdays.
- Faster preparation and delivery times were associated with higher customer ratings.
- Opportunities exist to improve delivery efficiency, customer engagement, and average order value.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing and partnership efforts on high-demand cuisines.
- Encourage higher order values through promotions and combo offers.
- Optimize rider allocation and delivery operations during peak demand periods.
- Increase customer engagement through ratings and feedback initiatives.
- Use data-driven monitoring to improve operational performance continuously.
